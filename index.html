Gymnastics in Ogden, Utah

Non-Competitive Gymnastics

Located in Ogden, Utah. Inspire gymnastics is a non-competitive gym coaching classes from walking to 18+ years old.

Who We Are

Shelly

I've grown up living and breathing gymnastics. I have a great passion for it and that is why I wanted to create a place for everyone to be able to participate and I could share my love of gymnastics with. I have been around gymnastics since 1992 and have a lot of experience that I want to share with those who come to the gym.

Sandy

As a mother involved in gymnastics for more than 15 years I can relate to the experience of parents and children a like. This helps make us adapt to the different people who choose to come to our gym. We are always willing to go above and beyond to make sure that everyone can learn in whatever way works for them.
