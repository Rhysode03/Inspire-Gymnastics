About Our Classes

Children's Fitness

Parent and tot class - walking to 3 years old.

A coach will lead the students through basic gymnastics skills. In this structured class, kids will learn gymnastics, following instructions and interacting with others. Each student requires an adult to accompany them through the class.

Preschool - 3 and 4-year-olds

These little gymnasts will learn the same basic skills that the big kids learn. Small size classes (6 kids) will assure that all will get hands-on attention from the coach to assure proper form is learned.

Kindergarten

Moving up from small cats or just joining our gym family the kids will be expanding on the basic skills of gymnastics. Coaches will be teaching the kids to have body awareness and form.

In this class, students work on all the different skills, modified to their ability while still being challenged.

At these levels, each student has a skill sheet. This assures that the students learn all the skills need to progress up through the levels safely. Classes limited to 8 students.
